### Changed

- Status bar settings now sync across your devices, and your agents can change them for you. Which items show in the status bar (directory, git, model, and the rest) used to be saved only in the browser you set them in; now they live with the rest of your settings, so a toggle you flip on one device shows up everywhere, and you can just ask an agent to hide or show an item. Your existing choices carry over the first time you open DorkOS after updating (DOR-431)
