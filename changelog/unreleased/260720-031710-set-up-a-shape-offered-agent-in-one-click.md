### Changed

- **"Set up" a Shape's suggested agent, and it arrives ready — not blank.** When a Shape offers an agent you don't have yet, the switcher's "Set up" now opens a single confirm card: meet the agent, read what it does in its own words, and see plainly what turns on, where it will live, and which skills it uses. One click creates it, already carrying its personality and its runtime. "Customize first" still lets you rename it before it's born, and "Not now" leaves the offer waiting for you. (DOR-355)
