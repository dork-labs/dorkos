### Added

- Report a bug or ask for a feature without hunting down your setup details. Open the command palette (Cmd/Ctrl+K) and pick "Report an issue", use the new help menu at the bottom of the sidebar, or run `dorkos feedback` in your terminal. DorkOS opens a prefilled GitHub issue in your browser with your version, operating system, runtimes, and on/off settings already filled in. You see and edit everything before you submit, and nothing is sent anywhere on its own. Only safe on/off values are included, never tokens, file paths, or anything from your sessions (DOR-292).
