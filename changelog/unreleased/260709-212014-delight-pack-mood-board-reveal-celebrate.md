### Added

- Agents can show a little personality now. Three new chat widgets: a **mood** face that blinks and emotes (eight feelings, and the celebrating one bursts confetti), a **board** grid for turn-based games like tic-tac-toe you can play right in chat, and a **reveal** that flips a coin, rolls a die, or shakes a magic 8-ball before showing the answer. Agents can also fire confetti on its own with the new `celebrate` command. Everything stays calm and honors your reduced-motion setting.
