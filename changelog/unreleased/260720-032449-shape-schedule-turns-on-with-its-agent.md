### Fixed

- A schedule that comes with a Shape — like a tick that checks your Linear inbox every 15 minutes — now switches on by itself the moment its agent exists. Before, if you set up the Shape before creating the agent, the schedule was made but left switched off for good, and nothing ever turned it back on. Now it finds its agent and starts running, whether that agent appears because you re-applied the Shape or simply created it, and a line shows up in your activity feed when it does. Schedules you made yourself are never touched — even one that happens to share a Shape schedule's name — and a schedule you turned off yourself stays off.
