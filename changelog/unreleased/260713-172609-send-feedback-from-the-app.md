### Added

- **Send feedback from the app.** A new **Send feedback** button in the help menu opens a small form to tell the DorkOS team what works, what does not, or what you wish it did — general feedback, a bug, or an idea. It goes straight to us and is sent only when you press Send. The **Report a bug on GitHub** and **Request a feature on GitHub** options are still there for when you want a public thread.
- A matching feedback form on the website at [dorkos.ai/feedback](https://dorkos.ai/feedback), linked from the footer.
- Feedback is not telemetry: it is a message you choose to send, so it ignores the `DO_NOT_TRACK` and telemetry switches. Those turn off tracking, not the Send button.
