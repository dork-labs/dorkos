### Added

- Celebrations got a lot more fun. There are now six confetti styles instead of one: a bigger multi-stage burst, aerial fireworks, side cannons that crossfire toward the center, a calm confetti drizzle, a golden star pop, and an emoji shower that throws whatever glyph you pick (🏆, ❤️, 😂, and friends). Agents can reach for any of them
- You can preview every celebration style in the Dev Playground's new Celebrations showcase — one button per style, plus a row of emoji presets

### Changed

- Confetti now bursts out of the button you clicked instead of always erupting from the middle of the screen, so a celebration feels like it came from the thing you did
