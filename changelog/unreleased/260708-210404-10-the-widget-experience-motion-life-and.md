### Changed

- Generative UI widgets now come alive as they render: a widget rises and settles into place, numbers count up (keeping their `$` and units), progress bars fill from zero, and charts draw themselves in — bars grow, lines trace, pie slices arc into place. Lists cascade in and highlight as you move over them, buttons give a little press when you tap them, and the loading state is a shimmering sketch of the card to come instead of a flat gray box. If you prefer reduced motion, every widget appears instantly in its final state.
