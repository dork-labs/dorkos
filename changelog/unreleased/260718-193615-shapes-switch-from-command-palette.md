### Added

- **Switch Shapes from inside the cockpit.** Open the command palette and pick "Switch Shape" to see every Shape you've installed and apply one in a click (DOR-355). Applying a Shape rearranges your workspace, turns on its extensions live — no reload — and offers its suggested agent for you to bring in or skip. If a piece is missing (an extension, an API key, an agent), you get an honest note about it instead of a silent gap, and "Reset to defaults" re-applies the Shape's own arrangement any time.
