### Added

- The right-panel button now shows a small count of how many things need your attention — stalled sessions, failed runs, and the like — and it ticks up live while the panel is closed, so you notice without opening anything. No items, no badge.

### Changed

- Pulse now updates the moment something happens: its Activity list refreshes off the live event stream instead of waiting on a timer.
- When the panel is showing only one thing, its header now names that tab (icon + title) instead of sitting there blank.
- Pulse hides a "View all" link when you're already on the page it points to — no more clicking a link that goes nowhere.
